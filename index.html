About
Malabar FOODS LTD

Malabar FOODS LTD is specialized in selling indian srilankan fruits, vegetables, groceries and cookware. Based in Slough, Malabar foods Ltd has built a global reputation in the production, imports, exports and wholesale distribution of quality products with high standards of customer service since 2014. Having untethered produces from Asia, Africa, Europe and America filling our basket for several years, we boast of half a million kg vegetable deals annually in UK. Recognising the need to sustain health and to help the next generation thrive robustly, we understand the inevitability of non- toxic diet in daily life.
